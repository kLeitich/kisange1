What We Do

Engaging community in working towards the liberation of women and gender and sexual minorities from patriarchal domination and economic oppression.
We are raising consciousness by creating conversation platforms to root out the ideological domination that leads to discrimination, oppression, and exploitation.
We are also working with people and organizations who share similar goals for liberation.

Our Mission

We are feminists and Socialists grounded in decoloniality.

Feminism and Socialism provide us with an ideological position to struggle for the liberation of women, gender and sexual minorities from patriarchal and capitalist domination, as we aim to achieve social and economic justice for everyone.

Our solidarity lies with those on the fringes of society suffering from multiple forms of oppression and whose lived realities are already resistance to oppression.
